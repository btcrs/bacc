Why?

I find a lot of value in gardening

Gardening is hard

Open Agriculture

  • Global population will be over 9 billion by 2050
  • We're using most of the arable land and too much water
  • Food production is not distributed among all food eaters

Hydroponics

Growing plants suspended in an inert medium without soil by using mineral nutrient solutions in a water solvent

Nutrient Film Technique

  • Semi-closed system
  • Higher yields
  • Can grow anywhere

Issues

  • Requires constant monitoring and maintenace
  • Succeptible to failure and disease
  • It's only semi-closed

Victor can help!

  • Automates monitoring and maintenace
  • Can adapt to any installation
  • Provides a quick, pretty view into the state of the garden

Design Criteria

Victor should be Scalable, Extensible, and Secure

What?

  • Victor is a framework
  • Service oriented architecture
  • Parts are swappable

Services

  • Container-Gardening

Container-Gardening

Deals with taking measurements, reporting them to the database, and controlling active components

Gardeners-Log

Handles all CRUD operations for the database

Gardeners-Shed

Mediates communication between users and the garden

Victor

Uses the other three services to provide a valuable user experience
