Building Monitored, Secured, And Self-sustaining IOT Hydroponic Gardens

Why?

Gardening is hard

IoT is popular

The population is big

As it turns out, keeping a garden is hard. At the very least I'm bad at it. IoT is a pretty big field already, and it's growing dramatically. There's a lot of change in the industry that I expect will happen over the next few years because there are a few major problems holding IoT back. Data aggregation through IoT sensors is capable of helping solve big problems. The population is still growing rapidly and a set up like the one I'm about to show could help feed them.

What?

Victor is a framework

Focus on extensibility and security

Primarily deals with data aggreagation

I put a lot of focus and effort into making Victor extensible. For that reason, I consider the project to be a framework rather than a standalone project. I've made some opionated choices throughout, but they're very changeable.
